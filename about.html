I am Muntaka Maliha Rahman, a Cyber Security Engineering student at the University of Frontier Technology, Bangladesh. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and defense — understanding how systems can be exploited in order to build stronger protections against those very threats.

My academic journey is deeply complemented by practical, hands-on experience. I work regularly in lab environments using industry-standard tools to analyze network traffic, probe vulnerabilities, and simulate real-world attack scenarios in controlled settings.

"The best defenders think like attackers — understanding threats is the first step to dismantling them."

Beyond technical skills, I am passionate about security automation — scripting Python and Bash tools that streamline penetration testing workflows and make security analysis faster and more reproducible.

I am actively seeking internship and project opportunities where I can apply my growing knowledge and contribute to impactful cybersecurity initiatives. I am always open to professional connections and collaborations aimed at enhancing digital security.
